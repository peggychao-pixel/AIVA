INTEGRATE MAXIMIZER / SATISFICER MODE INTO THE PRODUCT WITHOUT DRIFTING THE POSITIONING

Please integrate the maximizer vs satisficer insight into the existing product, but do NOT let the app drift into a generic decision-making tool, personality quiz, productivity app, or anti-perfectionism app.

==================================================
1. PRODUCT POSITIONING MUST STAY THE SAME
==================================================

This product is still:
- a lightweight, moment-based support tool
- for eating-related loops
- unfinished endings
- anticipatory panic
- nighttime search / repair mode
- emotional landing and closure problems

It is NOT:
- a general decision-making app
- a maximizer/satisficer personality app
- a productivity app
- a rational choice coach
- a generic perfectionism reduction app

The maximizer/satisficer insight should deepen the current product,
not replace its identity.

==================================================
2. HOW TO USE THIS INSIGHT
==================================================

Use maximizer/satisficer as an internal lens for certain moments.

The relevant user pattern is:

The user may not just be searching for the best option.
She may be treating “best” as emotionally safer than “good enough.”

For this user:
- “not best” can feel like “not safe”
- “good enough” can feel risky
- endless search may function as safety-seeking, not greed
- stopping the search may feel dangerous because it risks regret, unfinishedness, or a failed ending

This means the product should help the user:
- notice when best-seeking is really safety-seeking
- separate “best” from “safe”
- move from endless search toward a believable safe-enough state
- stop treating non-optimal as emotional danger

==================================================
3. DO NOT EXPOSE THEORY LANGUAGE TOO DIRECTLY
==================================================

Do not make the user-facing copy say things like:
- You are a maximizer
- Switch to satisficer mode
- You have maximizer tendencies

That is too theoretical and drifts the product.

Instead use natural product language such as:

Traditional Chinese:
- 你現在明明已經有一個可以的版本，但還是不敢停
- 你現在不是只在找更好的，可能也是在找更安全的
- 對你來說，「夠好」現在還不夠安全
- 你不是太挑，可能是現在停下來的代價感太高

English:
- You may already have an okay option, but it still doesn’t feel safe enough to stop.
- You may not just be looking for better — you may be looking for safer.
- “Good enough” may not feel safe enough right now.
- This may not be pickiness. It may be that stopping feels too risky.

==================================================
4. WHERE THIS BELONGS IN THE PRODUCT
==================================================

Use this logic only in moments like:
- endless search for a better option
- worth-it / expensive spirals
- pre-meal failure-prevention mode
- “I already have an okay option but I still can’t settle”
- “I keep looking for one more thing to make the night land”

Do not turn this into a global app mode.
Do not add a front-page maximizer test.
Do not center the whole app around this framework.

==================================================
5. FEATURE DIRECTIONS
==================================================

Please integrate this insight into small, product-fit features such as:

A. Detection prompts
Examples:
- 我明明已經有一個可以的選項，但還是停不下來
- 我一直在找更好的版本
- 我怕「夠好」之後會變成後悔
- I already have an okay option, but I still can’t settle
- I keep looking for something better
- I’m scared “good enough” will turn into regret later

B. Clarifying prompts
Examples:
- 你現在還有新資訊進來嗎，還是在重複比較？
- 你現在是在找最好，還是在找一個夠安全的版本？
- Are you still getting new information, or repeating the comparison?
- Are you looking for the best, or for something safe enough to stop on?

C. Safe-enough transition
The product should help the user shift from:
best-seeking → safe-enough landing

without sounding like “just settle.”

==================================================
6. STOP LINE DIRECTIONS
==================================================

Good stop lines:
Traditional Chinese:
- 不是最佳，不等於不安全。
- 夠好，不等於會失敗。
- 我現在可以停在一個夠安全的版本，不用找到完美版本。
- 我現在不是在找最好，我是在找能落地的版本。

English:
- Not best does not mean unsafe.
- Good enough is not the same as danger.
- I can stop at a version that is safe enough, even if it is not perfect.
- I may not need the best option to have a survivable ending.

==================================================
7. KEEP THE PRODUCT FOCUSED ON LANDING
==================================================

Always connect this logic back to the product’s real job:
helping the user reduce loops and reach closure / enoughness / emotional landing.

Do not let this become abstract optimization coaching.
It should always answer:
How does this help the user stop searching and land the night more safely?
