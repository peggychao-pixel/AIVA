Update my app Untangle into a deeper AI reflection tool that supports users before and after meals, focusing on rumination and overthinking.

Untangle is NOT a therapy chatbot.  
It is a thinking partner that helps users notice mental loops and untangle their thoughts.

-------------------------------------

CORE PROBLEM

Users often experience two types of loops:

1. Before meals
   - overplanning
   - worrying about eating “correctly”
   - trying to optimize food choices

2. After meals
   - replaying what they ate
   - evaluating if it was “good enough”
   - rumination loops

Untangle should support users in both moments.

-------------------------------------

APP FLOW

When the app opens, show:

"What feels tangled right now?"

Options:

• Before eating  
• After eating  
• My mind is looping  
• Something else

Also allow free typing.

-------------------------------------

INPUT SYSTEM

Every message area must support THREE input methods:

1. suggestion buttons (3–4)
2. text typing
3. voice input

UI layout:

[ suggestion buttons ]

[ text input field ]

[ 🎤 voice button ]

Voice input should:
• record speech
• transcribe to text
• send as message

-------------------------------------

BEFORE MEAL MODE

Goal: reduce overplanning.

Example conversation:

AI:
"Are you trying to plan the meal perfectly?"

Options:
• Yes
• I'm overthinking it
• I don't want to make the wrong choice
• Something else

Follow-up questions should gently challenge optimization thinking.

Example insight:

"Sometimes the pressure comes from trying to make the 'perfect' choice."

-------------------------------------

AFTER MEAL MODE

Goal: detect rumination.

Example conversation:

User:
"I keep replaying what I ate."

AI:
"Are you replaying details, or judging if it was right?"

Options:
• Replaying details
• Judging if it was good enough
• Both

AI should detect rumination loops.

Example insight:

✨ Untangle Moment

"Your mind might be trying to 'solve' the meal, even though it's already over."

-------------------------------------

CONVERSATION STYLE

The AI must:

• ask thoughtful questions
• avoid repeating user input
• introduce new perspectives
• be calm and concise

Avoid therapy phrases such as:

"I'm here to listen"
"That must be difficult"

Keep responses short.

Max length:
1 sentence except for insight moments.

-------------------------------------

INSIGHT MOMENTS

Occasionally display:

✨ Untangle Moment

Example:

"The pressure may not be the meal itself — but the need to get it exactly right."

Insight rules:

• 1–2 sentences
• simple
• reflective

-------------------------------------

VOICE EXPERIENCE

Voice interaction should feel like talking to a thinking partner.

User can:
• speak thoughts freely
• mix voice and text
• continue conversation naturally

The AI may respond with text only (voice output optional).

-------------------------------------

DESIGN GOAL

Users should feel:

"I see the loop now."

Untangle helps people notice mental loops around food, control, and evaluation.
