AFTER-EATING OPTION OVERLAP FIX + BRANCH LOGIC FIX

There are two major problems in the current after-eating guilty/overate flow.

==================================================
1. DO NOT OFFER OVERLAPPING BODY-STATE OPTIONS
==================================================

Current options include things like:
- 我身體已經夠了，心裡還在慌
- 我很撐，開始怪自己

These are too close and feel overlapping.

From the user’s perspective, “very full” and “my body has had enough” are not cleanly distinct enough to justify separate parallel options.

The real difference is not body fullness.
The real difference is the dominant emotional stuck point:
- panic
- self-blame
- unsatisfaction
- inability to close the moment

New rule:
After-eating options should differentiate by dominant loop mechanism,
not by slightly different fullness wording.

==================================================
2. REWRITE THE AFTER-EATING FOLLOW-UP OPTIONS
==================================================

Replace overlapping options with these more distinct ones.

Traditional Chinese:
- 我身體已經夠了，但心裡還停不下來
- 我現在最強的是罪惡感，開始怪自己
- 我沒有真的被滿足，只是一路吃過頭
- 最難的是這件事收不掉

English:
- My body has had enough, but my mind still won’t stop
- The strongest thing right now is guilt — I’m starting to blame myself
- I don’t feel satisfied; I just kept going too far
- The hardest part is that this moment won’t close

These options are better because each one points to a different intervention path.

==================================================
3. DIRECT USER SELECTION MUST ADVANCE THE BRANCH
==================================================

If the user selects:
「我很撐，開始怪自己」
or its rewritten equivalent

the system must NOT respond with:
“What part keeps pulling you back?”

That is too generic and ignores the user’s actual answer.

Instead, route directly to the self-blame / guilt subtype.

Example insight directions:

Traditional Chinese:
- 你現在最重的，不只是撐。
  是你一感覺過頭了，就開始把自己判錯。

- 最折磨你的可能不是這一口口吃下去的東西，
  是撐住之後，你開始用很兇的方式對自己說話。

- 你現在卡的，不只是吃多了。
  是身體一不舒服，自責就馬上接手了。

English:
- What feels heaviest may not just be the fullness.
  It may be how quickly self-blame takes over once you feel you’ve gone too far.

- The hardest part may not be what you ate.
  It may be how harshly you start speaking to yourself once your body feels overfull.

==================================================
4. NO GENERIC FOLLOW-UP AFTER A SPECIFIC USER CHOICE
==================================================

Once the user picks a specific emotional option,
do not revert to broad abstract prompts.

Bad:
What part keeps pulling you back?

Good:
Respond directly to the selected branch.

==================================================
5. LANGUAGE CONSISTENCY
==================================================

This screen must also obey one-screen-one-language.
No mixed English follow-up with Chinese prior state.
