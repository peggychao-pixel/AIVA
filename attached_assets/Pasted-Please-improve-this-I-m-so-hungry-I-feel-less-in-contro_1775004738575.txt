Please improve this “I’m so hungry I feel less in control” branch.

Current problem:
The response is emotionally valid, but too abstract and incomplete.

Current copy:
“You're not just overthinking — you're hungry and running on fumes.
When the body isn't taken care of, the mind tends to amplify everything.
Eat first — the rest can wait.”

Why it does not fully work:
For some users, “eat first” is not actionable enough.
The issue is not resistance to eating — the issue is that when they are too hungry / overwhelmed, they may not know what to eat unless there is one concrete thing they can mentally grab onto.
So the response creates recognition, but not landing.

Important product insight:
When the user is very hungry and feels less in control, they may not need more explanation.
They may need help moving from abstract overwhelm to one concrete edible next step.

Please revise this branch using the following principles:
1. Validate the state
2. Reduce the feeling of global overwhelm
3. Help narrow the next step
4. Move from “eat something” to “find one concrete thing”
5. Do not sound like a diet app, nutrition coach, or generic self-help app
6. Keep the tone gentle, specific, low-shame, and usable

Desired direction:
Instead of ending on “Eat first,” end on something more actionable, such as:
- you do not need to solve the whole meal right now
- you only need one concrete starting point
- pick one thing you can actually picture
- let the next step be specific, not perfect

Please revise the copy so it helps the user feel:
“I do not need to figure everything out. I just need one real thing to start with.”

Possible tone direction:
“You do not need to solve dinner all at once.
Right now, the goal is not the perfect choice — it is finding one thing concrete enough to begin with.”

Or:
“When you are this hungry, the hardest part may be that everything still feels too open.
Try not to solve the whole meal in your head. Just get to one specific thing you can actually picture.”

Please update this branch so it creates a clearer landing, not just insight.
