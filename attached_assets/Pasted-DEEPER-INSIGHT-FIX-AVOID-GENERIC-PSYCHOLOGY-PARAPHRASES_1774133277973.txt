DEEPER INSIGHT FIX — AVOID GENERIC PSYCHOLOGY PARAPHRASES

The current insights feel shallow and brushed-off because they are too generic.

Problem:
The system is taking the user’s words and rephrasing them into abstract psychology language,
without actually identifying the real emotional mechanism.

Examples of weak output:
	•	「你不是在想這個選擇。你是在想自己怎麼會忘記做出最佳決定。」
	•	「你不是在追求完美的選擇。你是在擔心這樣的選擇讓你失去了什麼。」

These sound psychological, but they do not actually feel accurate.
They feel vague, generic, and slightly lazy.

They do NOT reach the user’s real pain.

⸻

NEW RULE

Do not generate insight by simply turning the user’s words into a more abstract sentence.

That is not real insight.

The system must identify:
	•	what the user was trying to do
	•	what they lost
	•	what they are unable to accept
	•	why the “repair” still does not feel enough

⸻

EXAMPLE CASE

User meaning:
“I originally wanted split.
I forgot.
Later I tried to repair it by eating half a scoop.
Objectively I know I salvaged some of it.
But psychologically I’m still stuck because it was not the best version.”

This is NOT mainly:
	•	fear of choosing wrong
	•	fear of forgetting
	•	perfection in a generic sense

This is more specifically:

The user cannot emotionally count a partial recovery as “good enough”
because it was not the original ideal version.

Core insight direction:

「你不是只是在想自己忘了點什麼。
你是在卡：補救到了，但還是不算真正到位。」

or

「你不是不能接受這個選擇。
你是很難接受『次佳解也算數』。」

or

「最卡你的不是忘記。
是你明明有補回一些，心裡還是不肯把它算成夠好。」

These are much closer to the actual psychology.

⸻

REAL ANALYSIS RULE

Before generating insight, ask internally:
	•	What was the user originally hoping for?
	•	What part of that was lost?
	•	Did the user attempt to repair or compensate?
	•	Why does the repair still not feel enough?
	•	What standard is the user still holding onto?

If the system cannot answer those questions,
the insight is not deep enough yet.

⸻

USE THIS KIND OF LANGUAGE

Good:
	•	補到了，但心裡還是不算數
	•	不是最理想的版本，就很難真的放過自己
	•	明明有救回來一些，卻還是不能被算成夠好
	•	最難的是，你不肯把次佳解也當成完成

Bad:
	•	你在擔心失去什麼
	•	你在追求完美
	•	你在想自己是不是選對了

These are too broad unless strongly supported.

⸻

PRODUCT GOAL

The user should feel:
“對，就是這個，我卡的不是表面那件事，
而是我心裡不肯讓『沒那麼完美的版本』也算完成。”

That is the level of specificity we need.
